Home Screen

The home screen displays the quests or tasks that the player has selected to work on. It also provides information about the current events the player is participating in, showcases recently acquired achievements, and offers a glimpse into the guild, comprising peers within the course.

Group Chat

The group chat feature is closely tied to the tasks that players can complete to earn experience points (XP). It serves as a platform for players to join groups and engage in discussions related to specific themes or collaborate on tasks. Additionally, it provides a casual space for players to chat and socialize, fostering a sense of camaraderie and enjoyment.

Events

The events page displays the currently available events, most of which are created by the players themselves. These events primarily revolve around the competitive dueling aspect of the game.

Achievements

The achievements page serves as a comprehensive list of all the achievements acquired by the player.

Tasks

The tasks page is divided into three distinct sections, each catering to specific types of tasks: assignments, lectures, and discussions. A prominent circular icon with an arrow indicates to the player that they can interact with it. In the final product, this feature would provide detailed task descriptions, add the tasks to the player's list, and update their character's XP upon task completion for progression.

Duels

In this game, players take on the roles of characters located within a virtual dungeon. Player avatars are situated in the lower-left corner of the interface, while the dungeon master's presence is in the upper-right corner. As the game begins, the first player is presented with a question initiated by the dungeon master.

Players' responses are based on the 'spells' they've acquired as they progress through the leveling system. For example, if the dungeon master presents a question related to brainstorming, players must select an appropriate 'spell' from their arsenal to respond. Correct answers enable the game to advance to the next player's turn, while an incorrect response results in the entire group losing health.

1v1 - Start

During the planning phase, the initial concept was to allow players to select a specific category to focus on within the game. However, as the development process evolved, it became more logical to grant players access to all categories available for their character.

This approach ensures that the player's level and accumulated knowledge are significant factors during the dueling encounters.

Dungeon

This final image depicts how a dungeon operates, featuring a group of five peers engaged in a similar gameplay experience, often challenging each other. The game round initiates with the player on the far left and progresses to the next player once they have provided their response.
