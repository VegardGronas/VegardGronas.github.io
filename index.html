Home Screen

The home screen will show what quests or tasks the player has selected and should work on, as well as the current events they are a part of, and recent achievements acquired and lastly the guild, which is the peers in the course.

Duels

In this game, players within the dungeon are represented by images positioned in the lower left corner, while the dungeon master resides in the upper right corner. When the game commences, the first player is presented with a question from the dungeon master. The player's response hinges on the spells they've acquired through the level system. For instance, if the dungeon master poses a question related to brainstorming, the player must choose from their acquired spells to provide an answer. If the response is correct, the game proceeds to the next player. However, an incorrect answer results in the entire group losing health.

During planning the idea was that players could select a category they world focus on. However, after working more it made more sense to allow every category that the players have available to their character, so that the level difference and knowledge learned would be of value druing the duel.
